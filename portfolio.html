Controls Subteam

As a key contributor to the Chassis Controls Subteam for Bronco Baja Racing, I’ve worked on integrating electronic systems to monitor and control various aspects of our off-road vehicle. This includes:

  • Designing and wiring the power distribution box to supply and control subsystems
  • Developing a switch panel to toggle four-wheel drive and other key functions
  • Implementing sensors and displays for real-time vehicle data (e.g. speed, temperature)
  • Using a Raspberry Pi + Pico to interface with sensors and display information

This work has helped improve vehicle reliability and made debugging and testing far more efficient during competition prep.

3D Printing

I have a strong interest in 3D printing and regularly use it to support my design work and prototyping. I currently own a Bambu Lab X1 Carbon with the Automatic Material System (AMS), allowing me to print with multiple colors and materials efficiently.

This capability has helped me rapidly iterate mechanical components and validate designs before moving to final production.
